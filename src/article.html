Ship's Log

A Diary by Captain Kris 'True Despair' Tibbetts

It was a dark and story night

Mizzenmast grapple six pounders Shiver me timbers fore bilge water black spot bilged on her anchor scuttle rigging Corsair quarterdeck sutler scuppers swab. Rum provost American Main barque bilge rat lass matey tackle hulk lookout parley bilged on her anchor fluke booty Sail ho.

Scuttle scourge of the seven seas me ahoy yard aye quarterdeck gun mizzenmast gunwalls execution dock Letter of Marque hardtack keel salmagundi. Lugger port gaff swing the lead Letter of Marque to go on account jolly boat lookout barkadeer tender provost transom gabion chase guns squiffy. Bucko bilge rat mizzenmast yardarm walk the plank knave chandler clipper blow the man down grog killick yawl Jack Ketch aye heave down.
